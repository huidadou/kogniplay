Our Method

KogniMethodTM

A carefully designed cube-solving method that ensures everyone — regardless of age or background — can solve a cube and build lasting cognitive skills.

Why It Works

Teaching Philosophy

We teach understanding first, speed second. Our method adapts to the learner — whether they are 8 or 80.

Friendly Over Technical

Standard cube tutorials throw notation at you on day one. KogniMethod uses names you can remember and stages you can see, so anyone — child or grandparent — can follow along.

Mnemonic-First Learning

We use word associations, rhythm, and visual anchors to make algorithms memorable — especially important for older learners.

Intergenerational by Design

Sessions are structured so younger and older learners can progress together, each contributing and learning from the other.

Who It's For

Designed For Every Learner

KogniMethod is built for three audiences — each with their own adapted pace and materials.

👴

Active Seniors (40+)

Adapted pacing, larger visual aids, and shorter sequences. Focused on cognitive engagement and the joy of achievement.

🎒

Students

Structured progression from beginner to confident solver. Builds problem-solving and spatial reasoning alongside cubing.

👨‍👩‍👧

Families

Learn together. Our intergenerational format means parents, children, and grandparents can all progress in the same session.
